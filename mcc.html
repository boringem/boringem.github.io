Music City Center

Class Project.
A convention center wayfinding app.

About the Project

A prototype made as the final project for an experiential design class, to explore concepts of scale. This app is a wayfinding app for the Music City Convention Center in Nashville.

Features

My prototype allows users to view each individual floor of the convention center, as well as the overall floorplan. Users can select the closest bathroom, elevator, ATM, or dining option from the menu at the top, or search for a specific room.

Design

I chose colors that kept the tone for professional events in the center, but could also work for more lighthearted conventions. I wanted to make the design clean and modern, to match the interior of the convention center.

Iconography

I created an icon set that was consistent with the rest of the prototype, and chose the most important amenities that people would need in a convention center.
