Smart Shower

A shower experience that saves water, money, and time.

The average shower uses 2.1 gallons of water per minute.

Showering for twenty minutes, four times a week uses 8,736 gallons of water a year.

Cutting down by five minutes a shower saves 2,184 of those gallons.

So...what stands in the way?

  • A traditional timer does not allow users to view their history.
  • Calculating water usage is complex and time consuming.
  • The information needed is difficult to access.

How can smart home technology help?

  • A smart showerhead could capture all necessary data.
  • The app would handle all calculations, and the data would be personalized.
  • Information would be displayed in an easy to digest format, and be tracked over time.

The Process

I spent considerable time designing the onboarding process. When I was analyzing existing smart shower applications, I noticed that the setup could be confusing. Oftentimes, apps asked users to create their settings with no assistance. Many people don't know off the top of their head what temperature their shower is. I wanted to create an onboarding experience that helped users discover that information.

I also focused on the layout of the statistics section of the application. I needed it to be able to switch quickly between different types of data, but also be clear and easy to read. Because the water usage portion was the most important, I wanted to make sure that changes in water usage over time were very clear.

The Product

Icons and Buttons

These buttons are used during the onboarding process, when the user selects which statistics to keep track of. I used iconography on a card based layout to ensure that the options were clear.

Onboarding Process

I decided to use two onboarding processes. One allows the user to take a shower and records the temperature, duration, and water used during that shower. Following the shower, the user can edit the settings, or save it as a preference. The second has the user choose their settings manually, in case they don't want to have to take a shower to setup the app.

Statistics

The statistics screen allows the user to view the data that they have selected to track. Users can switch between views, or edit the statistics they want to keep track of.
